The Sentinel - the Guardian of Guardians. The Juggernaut in particular represents a Senex-influenced Sentinel, the guard of the known world who maintains the boundaries.

Juggernauts are those who stand against the unknown, the unmoving sentinels who maintain the peace. With inhuman [[Discipline]], Juggernauts are unmatched in extended combat, capable of withstanding the heaviest blows and pushing on without fatigue or hesitation.

UNIQUE:
Titan (P): Your first slot each turn is spend on the Titan active skill (Block, next 2 slots gain ATK/DEF/Integrity 1)
Acclimate: Permanently upgrade a slot to block a little when enemy attacks there
Behemoth: Taunt all enemies, scaling with times you fully blocked an attack this game

SYNERGIES:
[[Traits/Discipline#Reserves|Reserves]]: Upgrade a slot to restore HP/STA the next 5 times a combat skill is slotted there
[[Discipline#Stonewall|Stonewall]]: Block, then use the same slot next turn to block again
[[Discipline#Armor Plating|Armor Plating]]: Block, then gain Integrity 2
[[Discipline#Tackle|Tackle]]: Attack, scaling with your block
[[Discipline#Regimen|Regimen]]: Upgrade a slot to restore STA until you slot a different type skill here
[[Discipline#Composure|Composure]]: Upgrade a slot to gain ATK/STA here until an enemy contests and deals damage here
[[Discipline#Reckoning|Reckoning]]: Declare a powerful attack/block to happen during your 1st slot next turn
Steel Yourself: Gain DEF until you rest
