Judge - morality policing. Unlike the Mastermind, the Inquisitor believes their control to be just.

Discipline classes tend to be susceptible to attacks from other angles, but this is where the Inquisitor steps in to root out trickery. Armed with a cool head and honed training, this class excels at combining debuffs and defenses to corner and quash an opponent's assault. An Inquisitor's bread and butter revolves around exposing an enemy's strategy with their high [[Cunning]], and blunting its effectiveness with their high [[Discipline]]. As opposed to an Assassin, who respond perfectly to a single move, Inquisitors gradually defang their opponent.

Inquisitors excel at understanding a single target and completely neutralizing them. Detective allows Inquisitors to gradually expose an enemy's capabilities, so long as they accurately judge that enemy’s aggression. Too aggressive, and skills like Parry, Maneuver and Cover used on another ally may be needed to keep observing clearly. Too defensive, and skills like Disarm and Cover on yourself are needed to force some engagement. Pairing this with Profile, Inquisitors will soon see the lynchpins of the enemy strategy. Maneuver is a powerful defensive tool that scales with the Inquisitor's knowledge, predicting whether the next offense will be one big strike or a flurry of blows. Knowledge also improves Composure and, recursively, Detective, as attacks become easier to predict. Shackle and Disarm gives Inquisitors immediate respite from the most powerful plays, while Trial and Compromise gradually and permanently render the enemy ineffective. 

UNIQUE:
Detective (P): Each turn you combat an enemy without taking damage, they must expose a skill of your declared type (prioritizing unused ones first) (if no unexposed skills, they choose 1 to Lock 1)
Shackle: Lock 3 all of target enemy’s exposed skills of declared type
Trial: Downgrade all slots of an enemy by declaring a type/stat. Each time they slot that type, they lose STA. They can upgrade a slot back by permanently debuffing the stat you declared

DISCIPLINE:
[[Discipline#Cover|Cover]]: Target an ally to gain block and taunt
[[Discipline#Disarm|Disarm]]: Attack, Lock 3 the last attack the enemy used last turn
[[Discipline#Parry|Parry]] (T2: target enemy attacks you): gain big block
[[Discipline#Composure|Composure]]: Upgrade a slot to gain ATK and restore STA until you take damage from someone you combatted this turn

CUNNING:
[[Cunning#Profile|Profile]]: Expose all skills of one action type in enemy skillset
[[Cunning#Maneuver|Maneuver]]: Choose before committing - fully block the next attack this turn, or gain some block for all attacks this turn
[[Cunning#Compromise|Compromise]]: Choose a stat and target an enemy. They permanently debuff that stat a little and permanently buff their HP a little
